Our Focus

In a world dominated by screens, GAMAZE brings people back to real play. We create spaces where participants step away from electronics, live in the moment, and connect through physical, face-to-face gaming experiences.

Beyond fun, we emphasize fresh ways of thinking and spending time together, using play as a powerful tool to ignite personal and professional growth all while keeping the experience light, joyful, and energetic.

Meet the Founder

Monisha V Shankar

Monisha V Shankar

From the archery field to creating unforgettable gaming experiences

Monisha's journey began not in a boardroom, but on an archery field. What started as a regular Archery Summer Camp soon transformed into a deep passion. Within a short span, she went on to represent her state, win medals at the National level, and eventually represent India on a journey built on focus, discipline, and perseverance.

Her love for trekking, travel, and nature soon led her down another path — becoming a Trek Lead, where she organized and led treks across South India. These experiences strengthened her belief in learning through real-world challenges, teamwork, and shared experiences.

Choosing a profession that blended her passions, Monisha became an Outbound Instructor, designing and facilitating experiential training programs for organizations. Through these programs, she witnessed firsthand the powerful impact that activity-based learning had on teams and how play could unlock insights, strengthen bonds, and shift mindsets.

Every game carries a lesson, and learning is most effective when it feels like fun.

This belief became the foundation of GAMAZE – Get Amazed with Games. By designing physical, engaging games where learning meets excitement, Monisha created a platform that transforms ordinary moments into meaningful, high-energy experiences.

Today, GAMAZE stands as a reflection of her journey — rooted in passion, driven by play, and inspired by the joy of connection.
